Computer Science in Education

November 4 2024, by SOMEONE FILL THIS PLEASE

Computer Science has had a positive impact on education, as it allows students to make use of the Internet to access virtual learning platforms that allow them to develop their knowledge and understanding. The introduction of online learning during the COVID pandemic has allowed students to be able to learn even when they are not able to come to school. This technology can also be used to enable students living in remote areas who are unable to go to school to still receive the education they need.
